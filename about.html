About Kitari Consult

Kitari Consult Ltd is a management-consulting firm registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to provide premium advice on increasing productivity while optimizing returns through capacity development and planning.

The company provides premium advice to governments, institutions and high net-worth individuals on increased productivity to optimize returns through planning, cost reduction and capacity development. Our specialized skills give us competitive advantage in providing technical assistance and capacity development in a diverse range of fields for a wide variety of clients.

We are presently consultants and vendors to Aso Savings and Loans Ltd, Jigawa Savings and Loans Ltd., Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS), Kano State Government, Galaxy Backbone Plc. Etc. We have worked with MITA to design an e-governance curriculum for the office of the Head of Civil Service of the Federation, and have also trained three sets of federal permanent secretaries, directors of MDAs and Honourable Members of the House of Representatives in London and Dubai. We have over the years also provided services to Heritage Bank, Enterprise Bank, Fidelity Bank, Fintrust Microfinance Bank Ltd., Mouka Ltd. Etc. More recently we concluded training for FIRS staff on Excellence in Customer Service and also hosted a mortgage retreat for executive management of Jigawa Savings and Loans Ltd., in Abuja and Kaduna State respectively.

Under our Strategic Talent Development Consulting suite, we have complete contents (which we usually tailor to align with individual organization strategy, culture and need) in various skills and personality development areas.
