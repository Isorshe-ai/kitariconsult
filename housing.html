What We Do

Kitari consult has developed numerous residential and commercial facilities within and outside the country.

We are into property development, we carry out a wide variety of projects ranging from the restoration and re-lease of existing properties to the acquiring of raw land and the eventual sale of developed properties to clients and customers. We will carry out the basic activities; acquire land, finance real estate deals, build or have builders build projects, create, imagine, control and manage the process of development from the beginning to end.

In the course of our business operations, we will apply our resources to design and produce distinctive products and services that will ensure that meaningful value can be delivered on every project.

Kitari Consult seeks out investment property with latent value, and makes it available for its wide customer base. We strive to ensure that architectural design and real estate investment is affordable for any investor.
