Who We Are

Kitari Consult Ltd is a management-consulting firm registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to provide premium advice on increasing productivity while optimizing returns through capacity development and planning.

We provide premium advice to governments, institutions, and high net-worth individuals on increased productivity to optimize returns through planning, cost reduction, and capacity development. Our specialized skills give us a competitive advantage in providing technical assistance and capacity development in a diverse range of fields for a wide variety of clients.

Our Vision

We are in the capacity building and finance consulting business to provide unsurpassed services, advice, guidance and timely solutions to our clients, while creating reasonable return to our shareholders.

Our Mission

We will integrate human capital and cutting edge technology to strengthen public private partnership in the delivery of timely, comprehensive and accurate results to our clients.
