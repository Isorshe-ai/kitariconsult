Our Operations

Kitari Consult delivers value through three core operations:

Training

We offer courses specially designed to meet the training and development needs of all personnel in Government and Private Agencies. Multiple delivery methods are employed, including lectures, demonstrations, practicals, and workshops using real-life case studies.

Housing Finance

Kitari Consult is a major Housing Finance and Real Estate company. We have handled major projects, including the design of the Darmanawa Royal City Project, Kano, delivering over a thousand affordable houses within Kano metropolis.

Aviation Consulting

We are a leading authority in Aviation Consulting and Advisory, providing expert services to organizations such as AMCON and offering strategic advice on airline operations and status.
